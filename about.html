I'm Erick Mendoza, originally from San Francisco, California. For over 27 years, I've been fascinated by one question: why do people decide the way they do?

The Philosophy

Every buyer is navigating
an internal crisis.
Your job is to resolve it.

My approach to sales and communication is grounded in psychology: trust vs. doubt, confidence vs. competence, commitment vs. fear of loss.

Traditional sales training teaches you what to say. RAMP teaches you what the buyer is feeling, and how to respond with precision.

This isn't a system of manipulation. It's a system of understanding. When you understand what another person needs to feel safe enough to decide, you stop selling and start connecting.

The Framework

RAMPOC — A map of the
buyer's mind.

RAMPOC is not a sales script. It's a diagnostic framework that maps the stages of a buyer's internal crisis to precise intervention strategies.

At each stage of a sales conversation, your buyer is navigating a specific internal conflict. The RAMPOC framework teaches you to identify which crisis the buyer is in and respond with the strategy that resolves it.

This methodology is actively taught in university-level sales curriculum and applied in corporate training engagements across South Korea and internationally.
