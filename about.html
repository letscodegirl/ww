My Story

From commercial banking and real estate
to academia and consulting,
the question never changed.

Early in my career, I worked in commercial banking managing a portfolio exceeding $20 million. Later, I negotiated major retail real estate transactions, managed large operational teams, and advised business owners facing critical growth decisions.

Throughout those experiences, one pattern became impossible to ignore: people rarely make decisions for purely logical reasons. Behind every opportunity, objection, negotiation, and purchase is a psychological process.

That realization led me deeper into psychology, counseling, consumer behavior, communication, leadership, and influence science. Today, I combine real-world business experience with academic research to help organizations understand how people think, decide, trust, resist, and commit.

“I don't teach people how to sell. I teach them how people decide — and what to do when they don't.”

Philosophy

Behavioral science belongs in the
boardroom.

Most business problems are people problems disguised as business problems. Lost deals. Weak presentations. Difficult negotiations. Resistance to change. Leadership challenges.

The common denominator is human behavior.

RAMP exists to bridge the gap between behavioral science and business performance. The goal is not manipulation. The goal is understanding. When people understand what drives decisions, they communicate more effectively, negotiate more successfully, and build stronger relationships.

The RAMPOC Framework

A model for understanding the
buyer's internal journey.

RAMPOC was developed from years of professional sales experience, academic research, and the psychological theories of human development.

Rather than focusing on scripts or pressure tactics, the framework helps professionals identify the psychological barriers that prevent action and resolve them through trust, understanding, and strategic communication.

Today, the framework is used in corporate consulting engagements and university-level sales education.
